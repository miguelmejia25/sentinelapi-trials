üìñ Index Interpretation Guide

NDVI (Vegetation)

-1 to 0: Bare/water | 0-0.3: Sparse | 0.3-0.6: Moderate | 0.6-1: Dense healthy

NDSI (Soil)

Negative: Vegetation | Near 0: Mixed | Positive: Bare soil

NDMI (Moisture)

<0: Dry stress | 0-0.2: Moderate | >0.2: Good moisture

BI (Bare Index)

Negative: Vegetated | Positive: Exposed soil

BSI (Bare Soil Index)

<80: Dense veg | 80-100: Sparse | >100: Bare soil

CI (Color Index)

Negative: Dark/organic soil | Positive: Red/iron-rich soil
